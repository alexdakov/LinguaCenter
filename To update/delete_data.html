At Aura Learning, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal information. Use this page to understand how we handle your data and how to request a permanent deletion.

What deletion entails

Requesting a data deletion means your account, learning progress, certificates, and personal profile information will be permanently removed from our active servers within 30 days.

  • check_circle Permanent removal of account credentials
  • check_circle Loss of all course progress and badges
  • check_circle Immediate logout from all sessions

Before you go

We recommend downloading your learning certificates and transcripts. Once the deletion process is finalized, this data cannot be recovered.
